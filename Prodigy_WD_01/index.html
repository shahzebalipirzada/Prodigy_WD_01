Welcome to Tandoori Tales


Authentic Tandoori Delights


Experience the rich flavors of traditional tandoori cuisine at Tandoori Tales. Enjoy our succulent Tandoori Chicken, flavorful Paneer Tikka, fresh Naan Bread, and creamy Butter Chicken. Our dishes are crafted with authentic recipes and the freshest ingredients, all served in a warm, inviting atmosphere. Dine in or order online for a taste of tandoori luxury.

Our Menu

Chicken Biryani

A flavorful blend of aromatic basmati rice, tender chicken, and a rich mix of spices. This traditional dish is a celebration of flavors, perfect for any occasion.

Broccoli and Kale

A nutrient-packed dish with sautéed broccoli and kale, tossed in a garlic and chili-infused oil. Served on a sleek platter, it’s a modern twist on classic greens, perfect for health-conscious diners.

Masala Omelette

A fluffy omelette infused with finely chopped onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. A spicy and savory breakfast option, perfect to kickstart your day.

Zucchini Stir-Fry

Fresh zucchini slices stir-fried with garlic, herbs, and a hint of lemon. This light and refreshing dish is served elegantly on a white plate, showcasing the vibrant green colors.

Special Round Samosa

Crispy, golden pastry stuffed with a flavorful mixture of spiced potatoes and peas. This round twist on a classic samosa is a delightful snack with every bite.

Asparagus and Spinach Bhurji

Tender asparagus and spinach sautéed with onions, tomatoes, and mild spices. This dish is presented in a minimalist style, with the bright greens beautifully arranged on a square plate.
